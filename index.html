About Our Solution

Every year, around 600,000 stroke survivors experience significant challenges with walking, particularly due to difficulties in ankle control and strength. Current rehabilitation often involves restrictive boots, which prevent natural movement and rarely lead to long-term improvement.

Our innovative wearable rehab robot, The Strider, provides targeted support for the ankle, enabling patients to develop and maintain natural walking patterns. With consistent use, our technology aims to significantly improve walking capabilities and enhance the overall quality of life.

Key Benefits

  • Natural ankle motion and improved walking patterns
  • Enhanced strength and control over time
  • Greater mobility and independence in daily life
  • Long-term rehabilitation and recovery
